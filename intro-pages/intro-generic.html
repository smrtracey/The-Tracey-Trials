Welcome!

Thank you very much for being here. Whether you joined because you thought it sounded fun, because you were pressured into it, or because you didn’t fully read what you were agreeing to, you’re here now and there’s no backing out.

Over the next few months there’ll be all sorts of challenges thrown your way. Some will test creativity, some social shenanigans, some patience, and some will probably just leave you confused.

Given the variety of tasks, remember that you can choose which ones you do and don’t want to take part in, but the more you complete, the better your chances of winning.

Every challenge is different and you'll find some easier than others. I don't expect anyone to complete all the challenges but if you feel like there's some you can't do there's always other tasks and ways to get more points. Stay positive!

To get the most out of this, my advice is to properly get involved. Commit to the bit, back yourself, and don’t be afraid to make a little bit of a fool of yourself now and again.

Having a laugh, creating memorable moments, and creating some excuses to have fun is what this is all about. If there's any task you don't enjoy, no need to complete it.

Earn point however you can but remember that what's likely to earn you the most points are the moments that we'll be talking about after the competition is over.

Regardless of the outcome, just know that every challenge has been carefully designed with as little bias as I can manage.

good luck, play fair, and enjoy yourselves.
